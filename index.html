< 1．设计工具、方法、过程

设计工具：GitHub

方法、过程：

  1. 1 注册GitHub账号

    访问 GitHub官网，点击「Sign up」，填写邮箱、用户名和密码完成注册。

  2. 2 创建仓库
    • 点击右上角「+」号，选择「New repository」。
    • 仓库名称必须为 yourusername.github.io（yourusername为你的GitHub用户名），例如 xiaoming.github.io。
    • 勾选「Initialize this repository with a README」，点击「Create repository」。
  3. 3 上传网页文件
    • 点击仓库中的「Add file」，选择「Upload files」。
    • 拖拽本地已制作好的HTML、CSS、图片等文件到上传区域。
    • 点击「Commit changes」完成上传。
  4. 4 配置Pages服务
    • 点击仓库左侧边栏的「Settings」。
    • 在「Code and automation」部分找到「Pages」。
    • 在「Source」中选择「Deploy from a branch」，分支选择「main」，目录选择「/(root)」，点击「Save」。
    • 等待1-2分钟，页面顶部会显示访问链接

< 2．法庭意见书要素

Elements of Judicial Opinion in William Marbury v. James Madison, Secretary of State of the United States (5 U.S. 137 (1803))

01 Case Basic Information

  • Case Name: William Marbury v. James Madison, Secretary of State of the United States
  • Docket Number: 5 U.S. 137 (1803)
  • Court: Supreme Court of the United States

02 Factual Background

William Marbury was appointed as a justice of the peace for Washington County in the District of Columbia by President John Adams. The presidential commission was signed, but James Madison, the Secretary of State, failed to deliver it to Marbury. Marbury then petitioned the Supreme Court for a writ of mandamus to compel Madison to deliver the commission.

03 Legal Issues

  • Whether the applicant (Marbury) has a legal right to the commission he demands.
  • If Marbury has such a right and it has been violated, whether the laws of the United States afford him a remedy.
  • If a remedy exists, whether the remedy is a writ of mandamus issued by the Supreme Court.

04 Court Reasoning

On the Right to the Commission

The Court held that once the President signed the commission, the appointment was complete, and Marbury acquired a legal right to the commission.

On the Availability of a Remedy

The Court emphasized that the essence of civil liberty includes the right to legal protection when individual rights are infringed. Thus, Marbury was entitled to a legal remedy for the violation of his right.

On the Issuance of Mandamus

The Court reviewed the constitutionality of Section 13 of the Judiciary Act of 1789 (which authorized the Supreme Court to issue writs of mandamus). It concluded that this provision exceeded the Supreme Court’s original jurisdiction as defined by the U.S. Constitution, rendering it unconstitutional. In this analysis, the Court established the doctrine of judicial review—the power of federal courts to examine and invalidate acts of Congress that conflict with the Constitution.

05 Holding

The Court ruled that Marbury had a legal right to the commission and that his right was violated. However, the Supreme Court lacked jurisdiction to issue the writ of mandamus because the relevant provision of the Judiciary Act of 1789 was unconstitutional. Therefore, Marbury’s request was denied. Critically, the Court affirmed the power of judicial review, establishing that federal courts may declare acts of Congress unconstitutional.

句型 三、典型句型（5个）

①权利确认型

英文句型: It is the opinion of the court that [legal conclusion].

中文释义: 法院认为[法律结论]。

示例: It is the opinion of the court that Marbury has a vested legal right to the commission.

②法律冲突型

英文句型: If a law be in opposition to the constitution, [judicial decision].

中文释义: 若某法律与宪法相抵触，[司法裁决]。

示例: If a law be in opposition to the constitution, the constitution must govern the case.

③管辖权分析型

英文句型: The court shall have [type of jurisdiction] in [circumstances].

中文释义: 法院在[情形下]享有[管辖权类型]。

示例: The Supreme Court shall have original jurisdiction in cases affecting ambassadors.

④救济判断型

英文句型: Where a legal right is violated, [remedy availability].

中文释义: 当法律权利受到侵犯时，[救济是否可得]。

示例: Where a legal right is violated, the laws afford a remedy.

⑤先例援引型

英文句型: As established in [precedent case], [legal principle].

中文释义: 如在[先例案件]中确立的，[法律原则]。

示例: As established in this case, judicial review is a fundamental power of the courts.
